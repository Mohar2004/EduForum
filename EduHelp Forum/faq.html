Q1> What is the general timing of a semester?

Ans> Generally a semseter lasts for four to five months. It depends on the speed of teaching of the professor, number of holidays and many other factors.

Q2> What is the grading system of the institution?

Ans> The grading system is known as relative grading system where every students marks are compared and grades are given according to the number of students scoring within a certain marks range.

Q3> How many class-hours are there per day?

Ans> Generally there are two to three hours of practical classes per day and four hours of theory classes per day.

Q4> What are the venues of the classes?

Ans> The practical classes occurs in the respective departments and the theory classes occur in the New Lecture Hall Complex.
